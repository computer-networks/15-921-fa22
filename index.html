Overview

This is a weird seminar. It's not about doing research. Instead, it's about the (often, undiscussed) psychological, emotional, and philosophical themes that you will face as you come to identify as a researcher.

  • To reflect on why we do research in computer science.
  • To reflect on what makes research good or valuable.
  • To reflect on societal themes that impact what we study and how we study it.
  • To reflect on how becoming a researcher changes us as people.
